The Narrative

A Family Legacy

Founded by Peter Maina with a vision to bring farm-fresh quality to every urban doorstep, FFFARM began with just 50 hens and a commitment to zero-chemical feed. Today, we manage a thriving ecosystem of heritage breeds and organic pastures.

Our journey wasn't just about scaling numbers; it was about scaling trust. We've spent five years perfecting our "Hatch-to-Home" logistics chain to ensure that your products arrive with their nutritional profile fully intact.

Standards

Bio-Security & Ethics

We maintain a Tier-1 biosecurity rating, ensuring our flocks are protected from external pathogens without the use of aggressive antibiotics. Our birds enjoy a natural lifestyle that aligns with their biological needs.
