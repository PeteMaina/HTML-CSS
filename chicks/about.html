Our Story

Feathery Friends Farm is a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to providing our community with fresh, high-quality eggs and happy, healthy chickens. We started our journey 3 years ago with a small flock of hens and a passion for sustainable agriculture. Since then, our farm has grown, but our commitment to quality and animal welfare remains at the heart of everything we do.

We believe that happy hens lay the best eggs. That's why we raise our chickens in a humane and stress-free environment, allowing them to roam freely in our spacious pastures. We feed them a nutritious diet of grains, vegetables, and fresh herbs, ensuring they get all the essential vitamins and minerals they need to produce delicious and healthy eggs.
