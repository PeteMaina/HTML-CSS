Our Story

Feathery Friends Farm is a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to providing our community with fresh, high-quality eggs and happy, healthy chickens. We started our journey 3 years ago with a small flock of hens and a passion for sustainable agriculture.

We believe that happy hens lay the best eggs. That's why we raise our chickens in a humane and stress-free environment, allowing them to roam freely in our spacious pastures. We feed them a nutritious diet of grains, vegetables, and fresh herbs, ensuring they get all the essential vitamins and minerals they need to produce delicious and healthy eggs.

To buy or take a tour on our farm, you can reach us via the social links below. We also provide delivery services at a competitive fee.
