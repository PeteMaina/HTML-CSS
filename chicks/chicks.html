Hatchery

Hatch a World of Joy

Our family-owned hatchery implements a proprietary "Soft-Start" hatching protocol. By managing atmospheric pressure and light levels during the pipping phase, we ensure that our chicks emerge with higher energy reserves and stronger immunity.

Every chick is quality-certified and vaccinated before leaving our facility, giving you peace of mind and the best head start for your flock.

Brooding Excellence

Success in poultry starts in the brooder. Our experts have distilled decades of experience into these three pillars of early-stage husbandry.

Thermal Precision

Maintaining a steady 35°C (95°F) for the first week is vital. Our guides show you how to create a "warm zone" and a "cool zone" to let chicks self-regulate.

Immune Defense

We ship chicks with an initial electrolyte and probiotic boost already in their system, reducing "shipping stress" and promoting gut health.

Social Habitat

Light cycles play a huge role in growth. Learn how to manage resting periods to optimize bone development and reduce pecking behaviors.

Hatchery FAQ

What is the minimum order?

To ensure chick safety during transport (maintaining body heat), we require a minimum of 25 chicks for deliveries outside Nairobi.

Are they vaccinated?

Yes. All FFFARM chicks receive the standard Mareks and Gumboro vaccinations at hatch as part of our biosecurity commitment.
