Heritage

The Royalty of Poultry

Our birds are not your typical industrial breeds. We focus on slow-growing heritage lines like the Rhode Island Red and Sussex, which offer superior texture and a depth of lean protein that forced-growth birds lack.

Each bird is raised for a minimum of 84 days, allowing natural muscle development and a rich, authentic flavor profile to emerge.

Premium
Natural Diet

Zero Synthetics. Pure Vitality.

Forget artificial Growth Promoters. Our birds range on biological pastures, supplementing their organic grain with clover, insects, and mineral-rich herbs. This results in meat that's naturally tender and high in Vitamin A.

The Flocks

Breed Profiles

We maintain a diverse genetic pool of heritage poultry, each selected for their adaptability to the local climate and their exceptional contribution to our farm's ecosystem.

Rhode Island Red

The backbone of our farm. Known for their hardiness and incredible egg-laying capacity, these birds thrive in our pasture-rotation system.

Light Sussex

A dual-purpose classic. These birds provide exceptional meat quality while being surprisingly docile, making them perfect for our educational tours.

Black Australorp

The heat-resilience champions. Their vibrant black plumage and calm nature make them an integral part of our summer grazing groups.

A Day in the Life

Our birds follow the rhythm of the sun. From the early dawn forage to the evening return to their clean, spacious coops, every hour is designed for their comfort and safety.

  • 06:00

    Pasture release and first natural forage intake.

  • 12:00

    Hydration check and supplemental organic grain feeding.

  • 18:00

    Safe coop return and individual bird health spot-checks.
